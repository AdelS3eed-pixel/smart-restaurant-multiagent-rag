=== Booking Policy ===

Reservations can be made up to 30 days in advance through our staff or our digital assistant.
Groups of up to 8 people can be booked without special coordination. For groups larger than 8, please contact the branch directly at least 3 days in advance.
A booking confirmation is required 3 hours before the reservation time via text message.
Tables are held for 15 minutes past the reservation time. After that, the table may be released to walk-in guests.
Cancellations are free of charge if made at least 2 hours before the reservation time.

=== Refund Policy ===

Refunds are accepted within 24 hours of the order in case of a quality issue with the food.
To request a refund, customers should contact the branch directly with their order details.
Orders that have been fully consumed are not eligible for a refund, except in exceptional cases involving food safety concerns.
Refunds are processed within 3 to 5 business days after approval.
Delivery charges are non-refundable unless the delivery itself was at fault.

Nasr City branch: Every day, 8:00 AM to 11:00 PM. This branch opens earlier than the others to serve breakfast and healthy morning options.

=== Event Hosting ===

We host birthday celebrations, small family gatherings, and corporate meetings at our Tahrir and October branches. These branches have private seating areas that can accommodate groups of up to 20 people with advance booking.
The Shebin El Kom branch can host family events for groups of up to 15 people, subject to availability.
The Nasr City branch does not currently offer private event hosting, as its layout is designed for our health-focused daily dining experience.
To book an event, guests should contact the branch at least 5 days in advance to discuss catering options and seating arrangements.
A custom catering menu is available for events with 10 or more guests, offering a curated selection of our most popular dishes at a fixed price per person.

=== Loyalty Program ===

Customers can join our loyalty program for free at any branch or through our digital assistant.
Members earn 1 point for every 10 EGP spent.
Points can be redeemed for discounts starting at 100 points, equivalent to a 20 EGP discount.
Loyalty points do not expire as long as the account remains active with at least one visit per year.

=== Delivery Policy ===

Delivery is available from all branches within a 7 kilometer radius.
Minimum order value for delivery is 100 EGP.
Estimated delivery time is 30 to 45 minutes depending on the branch and order volume.
Delivery fees vary by distance and are calculated at checkout.
