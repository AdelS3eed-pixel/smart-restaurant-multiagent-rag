=== Our Story ===

Elsada Elafadel was founded in 2015 by three friends who shared one belief: that a great meal should feel like it was cooked by someone who actually cares about you. Adel Saeed, Saleh Ali, and Abdelrahman Emad met years earlier as engineering students who spent more time debating the best kebab in Cairo than studying for exams. What started as a running joke about opening their own restaurant slowly turned into a real plan, sketched out on napkins and late-night phone calls.

Adel Saeed took on the role of Executive Chef, bringing recipes passed down from his grandmother's kitchen and a relentless obsession with getting every dish exactly right, down to the last pinch of cumin. Saleh Ali became the Operations Director, the one who made sure that a good idea on paper could actually survive a busy Friday night service. Abdelrahman Emad took charge as the Brand and Business Director, the person who believed that a restaurant is not just about food, but about the feeling people carry with them after they leave.

=== The First Branch: October ===

The first Elsada Elafadel branch opened its doors in October City in 2015, in a modest space that could seat no more than thirty guests. The three founders worked every station themselves in the early months, from the grill to the cash register, learning firsthand what their customers loved and what needed to change. Word of mouth did the rest. Within its first year, the October branch had built a loyal local following, known for its grilled dishes and its warm, unhurried atmosphere.

=== Growing the Family: Tahrir and Shebin El Kom ===

Encouraged by the success of the original branch, the founders opened their second location in Tahrir in 2018, bringing the same menu and philosophy to the heart of the city. A third branch followed in Shebin El Kom in 2021, extending the Elsada Elafadel experience beyond Cairo and into Monufia, where it quickly became a gathering place for families and friends.

=== The Newest Chapter: Nasr City ===

By 2023, the founders noticed a shift. More and more regular customers were asking for lighter options, smaller portions, dishes built around grilled proteins, whole grains, and fresh vegetables instead of fried classics. Adel Saeed, who had personally started training for long-distance running that year, became the biggest champion of the idea: what if an entire branch was designed from the ground up around healthy, mindful eating, without losing the flavor that made the brand what it was?

That question became the Nasr City branch, opened in 2024 as the newest and most ambitious addition to the Elsada Elafadel family. Every dish on its menu was rebuilt from scratch with calorie awareness, lean proteins, and nutrient-dense ingredients in mind, while still carrying the same commitment to real, made-with-care cooking. It remains the newest branch to open its doors, and the one the founders describe as "the branch built for who we are becoming, not just who we've always been."

=== Our Signature Dish ===

Ask any regular customer what they order first, and the answer is almost always the same: our Macaroni Bechamel. Layers of tender pasta, seasoned minced meat, and a rich, golden-baked bechamel sauce have made it the most requested dish across every branch since day one. It is, in many ways, the dish that best represents Elsada Elafadel: comforting, generous, and made the way a family recipe should be made.

=== Our Slogan ===

"Cooked with care, served with soul."

=== Who We Are Today ===

Today, Elsada Elafadel operates four branches across Tahrir, October, Shebin El Kom, and Nasr City, each with its own character but all sharing the same founding promise: food that feels like home, made by people who still believe that's worth getting right.
